Sangkeun Park

Assistant Professor
I am an assistant professor in the Department of Software Convergnece at KyungHee University. My HCI research utilizes data-driven approaches to address various areas of Human-centered AI, Social Computing, and Intelligent System Design to improve the way people design systems and make decisions by exploring human behavioral patterns and designing novel intelligent systems.
Bio

I earned my Ph.D. in the Graduate School of Knowledge Service Engineering at KAIST in 2019, advised by Uichin Lee. My research interests span across various areas of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Specifically, I am interested in understanding human behavioral patterns on services and designing novel intelligent services.

Before Joining in KyungHee University, I was a senior engineer in the CX(Customer eXperience) team at Samsung SDS (2020-2022). I worked as a data analyst, responsible for analyzing user behavioral patterns to find out new insight to imporve user experience and innovative strategies for future goals for customers, big companies such as Samsung, CJ, and Starbucks.

Interest
